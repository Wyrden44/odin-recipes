Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto Pasta is an awesome, tasty dish you can make anytime you like, quickly. It takes such a short period of time and low effort to make this dish. Enjoy!

Ingredients (1 Person)

  • 180g whole grain pasta
  • Pesto
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add Water to a pot and add the paste. Let it cook for 15 mins. In the meantime, grind the parmesan cheese.
  2. Remove the water from the pot. Add the pesto and mix.
  3. Lastly, add the parmesan cheese on top. Add pepper to you liking.
