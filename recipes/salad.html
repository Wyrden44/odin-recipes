Simple tomato cucumber Salad

Description

This is a salad you can easily make and really eat with anything you like!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cucumber
  • 5 Tomatoes
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs Balsamico
  • 1 small blue onion
  • freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables and the onion in large chunks
  2. Put them in a bowl. Add olive oil, Balsamico, lemon and pepper.
  3. Enjoy!
